DAILY: AI Needs You

Stop using technology to replace your voice. Start using it to find the time to use your voice.

"AI has no heartbeat, no memories of the mist on Lake Norman, and no stake in our community. We don't use it to act human—we use it to clear the clutter so we can be human."

The "Why" Behind the Machine

The greatest threat of AI isn't that it replaces us, but that we use it to automate the very things that make life worth living. We handle the logistics so the human can handle the soul-work.

×

Wrong Way: Automation

Using AI to write a generic birthday card to a friend because you're "too busy." This creates a thinner, more hollow life.

✓

The DAILY Way: Reclamation

Using AI to organize your chaotic work schedule so you can leave at 5 PM and take that friend to dinner. This creates a more lived life.

The DAILY Practice

A framework for living a real life in a digital age.

D

Discover Needs

Where is the "life-friction"? Identify the dry, administrative tasks that are stealing your time from people and place.

A

Assist Reality

Let the AI solve the logistical puzzle—meal plans, itinerary drafts, data summaries—so you can physically show up.

I

Infuse Self

Keep your fingerprints visible. Use AI to sharpen your ideas, but never let it hide your actual voice or stories.

L

Listen Locally

Anchor the AI in Charlotte. Reference local neighborhoods and history to keep the output grounded in our community.

Y

Yield with Care

The final check is your responsibility. If it feels machine-made, you've leaned too hard on the tool.

Reclaiming Your Reality

Workshop Challenges

"Using the machine to find the human."

1

The "Un-Plug" Audit

Identify one administrative task currently weighing on you. Use AI to create an execution plan to get it off your plate so you can be present elsewhere.

Action Prompt

"I am feeling overwhelmed by [Task]. Give me a step-by-step plan to finish this in 20 minutes so I can go for a phone-free walk in Freedom Park. Then, suggest 3 local landmarks I should look for to stay grounded in the moment."

2

The "Local Soul" Filter

Challenge the AI to think like a neighbor. If its advice sounds like a generic robot, force it back into the reality of Charlotte.

Action Prompt

"You just gave me a generic answer. Rewrite this so it respects the culture of Plaza Midwood and includes a reference to our local pride in the 49ers. What part of your first answer would a real Charlottean find 'fake'?"

3

The "Voice Anchor"

Practice 'I' (Infuse Self). Never let the AI lead. You provide the heart; the AI provides the spine.

Action Prompt

"Here is a paragraph I wrote. It's rough, but it captures how I actually feel. Help me clarify the structure, but do NOT remove my story about [Personal Experience]. If you change my voice too much, I want you to explain why."
