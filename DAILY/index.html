Step 1: Define Your Friction

The first step is to Define the specific task. Don't look for an "AI problem"; look for a "Life-Friction." What is currently stealing your energy from your human priorities?

The Human Check: Does this task require empathy or nuanced moral judgment? If yes, keep it manual. If it’s just logistical weight, define it here.

Step 2: The Collaboration

Now, we Augment your capability. We aren't asking the AI to "think" for you, but to provide the structure for the task you just defined.

Your Dynamic Prompt

"I need to [Your Task]. Help me organize this into a 20-minute action plan so I can reclaim an hour of my afternoon for [Human Activity]."

Workshop Tip: Be specific about the time you want back. This anchors the technology in your actual lived reality.

Step 3: The Audit

The AI has produced a draft. Now, you must Infuse it with your voice and Localize it to your environment. Interrogate the result for generic "AI-speak."

Infuse Personality

Does this sound like you, or a brochure? Practice adding your specific "Interrogation":

"This sounds too corporate. Adjust this to reflect my specific neighborhood context in Charlotte and use a neighborly, direct tone."

Localize Facts

Check for local hallucinations. Ensure the locations and resources mentioned are actually in your community.

"Are these specific to [My Zip Code]? Ensure all logistics mentioned are achievable within my local transit or walking range."

Step 4: The Compass

"The final check is your Yield. You take the responsibility for the output, ensuring it serves your human flourishing."

Responsibility Audit

  • I Defined the task clearly from my own needs.
  • The Augmentation saved time without sacrificing quality.
  • I Localized and checked all the facts myself.

Responsibility rests with you. Always.
