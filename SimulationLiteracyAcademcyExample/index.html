Charlotte Context

Set near the historical intersection of Beatties Ford Road & West Trade Street, the simulation mirrors real-world challenges faced by Charlotte's historic West End regarding housing equity, gentrification, food security, and public transit.

CATS Bus Routes: #7, #8, #22 Local News
Crisis Event #1

The Threat to 'Beatties Ford Green & Cooperative'

A local community safe haven providing 34 affordable garden cottages and the corridor's largest resident-managed urban farm has just been issued a 30-day demolition notice by a developer to build the "Queen City Luxury High-Rise". Simultaneously, CATS transit line revisions are cutting the neighborhood's direct express route.
