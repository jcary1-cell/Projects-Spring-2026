Critical Literacy Failure

Scenario: The City Planning Commission is hosting a town hall regarding new zoning laws in the Eastway-Central neighborhood.

An intern, lacking AI literacy, relied solely on the operational functionality of an LLM (ChatGPT) to generate the community invitation. They accepted the AI's first draft without applying critical reasoning.

Because generative AI models are heavily trained on corporate and affluent datasets, the default output stripped the diverse neighborhood of its context, generating a highly biased, elitist document.

> CONSEQUENCE: Mailing this unchecked AI draft will alienate the working-class residents, suppress community attendance, and violate our ethical commitment to human-centered communication.

Your Task: You must reclaim human agency. Audit the AI's output, identify the harmful biases, and rewrite the message to serve the actual community.

The Core Lesson
