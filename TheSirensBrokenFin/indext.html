🐙 Ursula's Sea Witch Warnings (Red Flags)

While most broken toes heal nicely on their own with at-home care, a traumatic fracture can turn nasty if you ignore complications. Cleveland Clinic advises swimming directly to an emergency grotto (healthcare provider) immediately if you experience:

🥶
Circulation Cut-Off Sudden numbness, tingling, or if the fin turns abnormally pale or deep dark blue.
🌊
Swelling Surge A sudden, extreme spike in throbbing pain or swelling that persists after elevate & ice.
🩸
Open Reef Cuts Bleeding or an open wound extending down to the bone (open fractures require urgent care).
🌡️
Siren Fever Chills, fever, or red streaks branching from your toe, indicating a severe deep tissue infection.
🔱
Crooked/Deformed If the toe is pointing sideways, overlapping another, or looks visibly displaced.
⏳
Stuck at Sea No signs of improvement after several weeks. Remember: normal healing is 6 to 8 weeks.
