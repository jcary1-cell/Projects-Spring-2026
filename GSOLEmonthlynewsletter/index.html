President’s Message

Dear GSOLE Members,

As February arrives, I find myself thinking back with real gratitude to everyone who shaped this year’s conference. The event felt alive with generosity, curiosity, and the kind of intellectual honesty that marks GSOLE at its best.

Maha Bali opened the conference with “Creating Compassionate Learning Communities Online,” a plenary that invited us to think carefully about what equitable hospitality looks like in digital spaces.

To continue these discussions, I hope you will join us at the Annual Meeting on April 2, where we will look ahead to GSOLE’s next decade, and at our monthly Cyber Salons.

Best,
Amy Cicchino
President, GSOLE
