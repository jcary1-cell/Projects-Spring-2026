Meal Planning AI Guide

The Secret Sauce Prompt:

"I am uploading a photo of my fridge and pantry. Based on these ingredients, give me 3 dinner ideas that take under 20 mins. For each, provide a grocery list of exactly what I am missing."

Why it works:

Most AI models now have "vision." Instead of typing everything you have, just snap 3 photos. It reduces the 'decision fatigue' that happens at 5:00 PM.

Logistics & Calendar

Never manually create a calendar event again.

The Prompt:

"Here is a messy list of my meetings next week. Create a structured calendar view and provide a single-line link I can click to add each to my Google Calendar."

Pro Tip: If you use Gemini, you can just say 'Add these to my Google Calendar' and it will do it directly.
